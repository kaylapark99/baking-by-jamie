Hi, my name's Jamie and baking is my passion!

I live in Randolph, New Jersey, with my husband, three kids, Mom, and two dogs. My passion for baking started a few years ago, and after series of trying out recipes, cake designs, and sharing these assortments of goodies, I was hooked.

After working in corporate for the last 30 years, I am now pursuing my dreams to create these wonderful treats, and I hope to share the enlightenment with you. I am proud to say that I make everything at request with ingredients that are fresh, organic, and made without any preservatives. New designs and treats are added frequently as these are also posted on my Instagram.
